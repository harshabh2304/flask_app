Cafe Insights is a simple web application designed to help users discover and share information about cafés. It allows users to explore cafés based on location, power socket availability, opening hours, and ratings, making it easier to find the right place to work, relax, or meet. Registered users can securely log in and contribute new café details, helping build a useful and community-driven café directory.
